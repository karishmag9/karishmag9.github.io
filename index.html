Jill of All Trades

One day you want to do this. The second you want to do that. The third day you watch Man of Steel. You wonder if you were planted on Earth for a purpose too. You're motivated to do something beyond "this" and "that" (but, because you're YOU, you will begin preparations the next day). The fourth day (which was the day "jill" was to become a master) you've forgotten all about the first three days. Loop over these 4 days (just watch a different movie) to form all the years of your life. Well, at least that's how I'd summarize my life formation.

Now, the thing is that I am fully capable of doing "this" as well as "that". But there is ALWAYS something after which there is always procrastination. For me, 90% of the time this "something" is the Internet. So, I thought of bringing the two together and creating something that has a little bit of everything that "jill" can do. :)
